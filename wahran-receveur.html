Wahran Receveur Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Wahran Receveur is a restricted application for authorized drivers and fare collectors operating on the Oran bus network. It transmits a bus's progress to produce estimated arrival times for users of Wahran Transport.

Account data

Depending on how an account is created, Wahran Receveur collects a username or pseudonym. When a user requests an account through the application, an email address is required to follow up on the request. Full name, bus number, and bus model are optional. Accounts created directly by the administrator may operate using only a pseudonym. Authorized routes, service hours, and account status are managed to control access. An account must be approved by the administrator before it can transmit trip data.

Trip and location data

During an active service, the application may process the selected route and direction, reported stops, report timestamps, and the location, accuracy, and speed supplied by Android.

  • Location is used only after a trip has been started and Android permission has been granted.
  • A persistent notification indicates that GPS tracking is active.
  • Tracking can be stopped from the application or its notification.
  • If location permission is denied, manual reporting remains available.

Purposes

This data is used to authenticate authorized accounts, estimate bus progress, calculate estimated arrival times, improve travel-time estimates, and detect errors or abuse such as impossible locations or excessive submissions.

Public display and service providers

Passengers do not see the driver's or fare collector's identity, email address, or raw GPS coordinates. Only estimated arrival times and information required for the passenger service are published. Personal data is not sold or used for advertising.

The technical infrastructure is hosted by Cloudflare, which processes technical requests and stored data as a service provider where necessary to operate and secure the service.

Retention and security

Individual GPS reports normally expire after 10 minutes, and published active-location snapshots expire after 2 minutes without a new signal. Account data and certain security logs may be retained longer to administer access, prevent abuse, and investigate incidents. Communications use HTTPS, sessions can be revoked, and administrative secrets are not included in the application.

Access, deletion, and contact

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of account data, email transportalgerien@protonmail.com. Include the relevant account identifier so the request can be verified.

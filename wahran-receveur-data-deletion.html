Wahran Receveur Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how users of Wahran Receveur, published by Wahran Transport, can request deletion of their account or specific associated data.

Request account deletion

Email transportalgerien@protonmail.com with the subject Wahran Receveur account deletion.

Send the request from the email address associated with the account when possible, and include the Wahran Receveur username. Do not include your password.

We may ask for limited additional information to verify that the requester controls the account. Access and active sessions will be revoked when the request is accepted. Verified requests are completed within 30 days.

Data deleted with the account

  • Username or pseudonym and email address.
  • Optional full name, bus number, and bus model.
  • Authorized routes, service hours, account status, and active sessions.
  • Account-linked trip and location records that remain identifiable and are not already expired.

Delete specific data without deleting the account

You may use the same email procedure to request deletion or correction of specific optional profile information. Clearly identify the data concerned and state that you want to keep the account.

Retention after a request

Live location snapshots normally expire within 2 minutes without a new signal, and individual GPS reports normally expire after 10 minutes. Limited security and abuse-prevention logs may be retained for up to 90 days after account deletion, then deleted or de-identified. Data may be retained longer only when required for a legal obligation or an active security investigation.

Aggregated or de-identified transit statistics that can no longer be connected to the account may be retained to improve estimated arrival times.

OpenTregna Data Deletion Request

Effective date: 25 June 2026

This page explains how OpenTregna users can request deletion of data submitted through the OpenTregna app.

Developer contact: transportalgerien@protonmail.com

How To Request Deletion

Send an email to transportalgerien@protonmail.com with the subject:

OpenTregna data deletion request

In your email, include enough information to identify the data you want deleted, such as:

  • The city or wilaya where you submitted a contribution.
  • The approximate date of the submission.
  • The title or text of the contribution.
  • If available, a screenshot from the "My contributions" screen.

Data That Can Be Deleted

OpenTregna can delete user-submitted contribution data stored on the server, including:

  • Proposed lines.
  • Proposed stops.
  • Reports or correction notes.
  • Optional map pinpoints included in a submitted contribution.
  • The anonymous contributor identifier attached to those contributions.

Data Stored Only On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling the app:

  • Favorite lines.
  • Favorite stops.
  • Favorite addresses.
  • Recent trips.
  • Local app preferences.

Data That May Be Retained

OpenTregna may retain limited non-personal or aggregated records when needed for security, abuse prevention, debugging, or legal compliance.

Anonymous aggregated analytics do not contain account details, names, email addresses, phone numbers, device identifiers, or exact GPS coordinates.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are reviewed manually. OpenTregna aims to process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time after receiving enough information to identify the relevant data.
